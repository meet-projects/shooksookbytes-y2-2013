Who We Are :

We're a group of MEET students working on making a website for the old city of Jerusalem, where you can enter as a customer, a shopkeeper or a tourist and you can see the shops there and get more information about the place.
Shopkeepers, you can add your shop, add information about it and the items in it.
Customers and Tourists, you can find shops, in addition the tourists can find hotels, resturants and archetectural sites in the Old City and around it.
